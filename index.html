THE START OF YOUR BITCOIN JOURNEY

Four Tasks Before Beginning

KEEP IT PRIVATE

The main privacy issue with bitcoiners comes from them telling other people about their bitcoin holdings. KEEP IT QUIET, you do not need to share this information whith anyone. The more people know you hold bitcoin, the more exposed you are. Start practicing individual responsibility, a low profile will keep you under the radar.

BE AWARE OF SCAMS

The CONTROCANTO team will NEVER ask you for your ID, password or seed phrase. We will never recomend you to buy sell or make any transactions at any time. Always think twice before you provide your details. Your name, address, phone number and email are valuable assets for scammers. We do not need to know your real name, your address or your phone. We ONLY require an email address for communication to arange the course. Please use an alias.

NEW EMAIL

Please get a new email account now. We recomend ProtonMail, you can get a free account. Do not use your real name or provide any personal information. This will be the main email you will use from now on for everything related to Bitcoin. It will act as a barrier between you and your new Bitcoin identity. Use this email to comunicate with us.
