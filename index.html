A Few things before we start

Keep It Quiet

You are probably excited and want to tell everybody about Bitcoin. This is not a good idea. Privacy is essential with Bitcoin.

Be Aware Of Scams

We will NEVER ask you to buy anything or make any transactions. We will NEVER ask you for your ID, password or seed phrase.

New Email

Go to ProtonMail and create a new free account. Do not use your real name or provide any personal information. This will be the main email you will use from now on for everything related to Bitcoin. It will act as a barrier between you and your new Bitcoin identity.
